Cuddles on the sofa.

From the moments you were born I was there. You made me the proudest man I could be. We used to spend so much time playing around the apartment with all of your favourite toys. I devoted my life to you and you rewarded me everyday with your laughter and smiles.

At the aquarium.

As a family and as a single daddy we went to many places. We were always going to parks, to museums, to the shops, to special places like aquariums and theme parks. I did my best to expose you to the world and all the fun and beautiful things in it.

At the nursery.

It was my job to pick you up and drop you off at the nursery everyday. Everyday I had a little heart break leaving you there but I trusted your teachers and knew you would be happy there. I attended your open days, had good relationships with your teachers and cheered you on at Sports Day. During my single daddy days I would miss you so much during the week, when it was my turn to pick you up on Friday afternoon I would race to get to nursery after work, my whole body buzzing with excitement. I would give the world to have that feeling again.

Day out with friends.

This picture was taken not long before I last saw you. A good friend of mine drove us to a big theme park, we rode the attractions, ate ice-cream and enjoyed the sunny weather. You both wanted to be held and cuddled so much. We were a team, daddy and his boys, father and sons on a day out. I have so many photos of all our days out and fun times that we had together. The happiest moments of my life! But I know in my heart there are more to come. I love you boys, we will meet again.
